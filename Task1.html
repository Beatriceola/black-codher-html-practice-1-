Chimmamanda Ngoze Adiche's best selling books

  1. Purple Hibiscus

    2. Purple Hibiscus is a story of pain, love, freedom and family. The novel takes its reader down in history to a young Nigeria plagued by economic instability and political insecurity.

    Purple Hibiscus Audio Summary

  2. Half of a Yellow Sun

    3. Half of a Yellow Sun is a tale of history, pain, and the horrors of war. The story starts at the dawn of a new Nigeria free from colonialism. Ugwu, a young village boy, leaves his village for the throbbing city of Nnsuka, where he meets Odenigbo, a mathematic professor at the University of Nigeria.

  3. Americanah

    4. Americanah tells the story of two lovers, Ifemelu and Obinze, who are torn apart by the milirary dictatorship in Nigeria. With Ifemelu heading to the US and Obinze becoming an undocumented immigrant in the Uk, their paths get wider apart.
